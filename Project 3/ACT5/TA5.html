What Are the SDGs?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are 17 global goals set by the United Nations to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure peace and prosperity for all by 2030.

Key Milestone

The SDGs were adopted in 2015 as part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development by all United Nations Member States.

Learn More

Visit the official UN website to explore all 17 goals:

https://sdgs.un.org/goals .

How You Can Help

Reduce plastic use, support local businesses, save energy and water, and educate others about sustainability.
